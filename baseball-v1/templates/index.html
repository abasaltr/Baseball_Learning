Scope

For this project, we chose to analyze a data set from the "Modern Era of Baseball". The "Modern Era of Baseball" began in 1901 and can further be broken down into five smaller sub-era's. For this project, we decided to focus more on what has been dubbed as the "Steroid Era" and that era began in 1980.

Analysis

As a group, we decided to analyze team data and player data from the last forty years. We chose to use four different modeling techniques and compare the modeling outcomes. The idea was to take the historical data and predict team wins and future player statistics for the next couple of years. The idea being that, player statistics are directly related to a team's winning percentage. We ran a Linear Model, Lasso Model, Elastic Model, and a Ridge Model and then compared the results to previous years. In the visuals below you will see the correlation between the models.
